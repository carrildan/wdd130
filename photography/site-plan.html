Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to post and share whitewater rafting´s photo and video contents. It will post photos, videos of people having fun during the activities with the purpose of reaching out possible new clients who want to check prices, pictures in quality, region cover, inspiration and to contact the service. This website also look to reach out current clients that want to see their pictures and videos, share the adventure with friends and family and keep contact if they want to refit again.

Audience

This website aims young women who are about to have 15th birthday party and want a photograph and video essay, middle age women who have family and want make a family essay or take a professional profile picture (or a personal profile picture). Señor women who would like to get a couple or family essay, middle age men who want to take a professional profile picture (or a personal profile picture), couples who want a wedding party essay, pregnant who want to take a pregnancy essay and mothers who want to have a ‘smash the cake essay’.
